SSHx — Shared Terminal

Install sshx on your VPS to get a shareable browser terminal link. Anyone with the link can view or collaborate in real-time.

What SSHx does:
  • Installs the sshx agent on your VPS via SSH
  • Generates a unique sshx.io share URL
  • You can open it in any browser — no client needed
  • Supports multiple windows and real-time collaboration
Or run manually on your VPS: curl -sSf https://sshx.io/get | sh && sshx
